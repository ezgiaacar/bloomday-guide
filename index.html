01 — Tasks

The root of everything

Every seed you earn, every plant that grows, every streak you build — it all starts with completing a task. Tasks are simple, but they drive the entire economy of your garden.

1

Add a task

Tap the input bar at the top of the Tasks screen. Type your task and press ADD. You can assign a category (Work, Health, Home, Learn, Social, Self) to organise and filter later.

2

Set a time window (optional)

Tap Time in the input bar to assign a start time in HH:MM format, e.g. 09:00 or 14:30. Then choose a duration (15 min – 4 hours). Tasks completed during their time window earn a +1 bonus seed.

3

Set a reminder

Each task card shows a 🔔 icon. Tap it to receive a notification 5 minutes before the task's start time. The bell turns gold when a reminder is active. Tap again to cancel it — the notification is removed immediately. Reminders only fire if you have explicitly set one; there are no automatic background notifications.

4

Complete it — earn seeds

Tap the circle on the left of a task to mark it complete. You earn 2 seeds per completion. The base rate applies once per task per day — undoing and re-checking does not grant extra seeds.

5

Plan across days

Use the calendar selector above the task list to assign tasks to future or past dates. Tap any day to view or add tasks for that date. Today's tasks always appear first.

◉

One reward per task. Seeds are tied to the first completion. Undoing a task and re-checking it on the same day gives zero additional seeds. This prevents exploitation of the economy.

03 — Plants

Species, growth & full bloom

Each plant has a maximum growth value it must reach. Growth comes exclusively from watering — completing tasks earns seeds, not growth. Every species has its own personality, trivia, and a poem that surfaces when it reaches full bloom.

Growth

How plants grow

Plants grow through watering alone. Completing tasks earns you seeds to buy more plants — it does not directly affect growth. To grow a plant, you need to tend it consistently each day.

  • Watering adds +2 growth directly
  • Sunny weather adds a +1 bonus on your next watering
  • Outdoor plants in rain get free watering for the day
Watering

Watering your plants

Each plant can be watered once every 20 hours. Watering from the garden scene or the plant strip both work. Missing a day causes mood to drop — wilting plants grow slower.

  • Watered today → Thriving (gentle glow, sway)
  • 1 day missed → Happy
  • 2 days missed → Thirsty
  • 3 days → Wilting
  • 4+ days → Critical (red warning banner)
Full Bloom

The bloom moment

When a plant reaches 100% growth for the first time, a modal rises from the bottom of the screen. It shows the plant at full illustration, a brief poetic line specific to that species, and your reward: +20 seeds and +1 Resilience Token. This happens only once per plant. Re-purchasing and re-growing the same species does not trigger it again.

04 — Focus Mode

A commitment contract

Focus Mode is not a UI dimmer. It is a deliberate constraint: you set a timer, lock out task-adding, and earn +1 bonus seed for every task you complete during the session.

How to use it

Below your task list, you will see the FOCUS button and four duration options: 25 min · 45 min · 60 min · 90 min. Select a duration first, then tap FOCUS to start. The button turns gold and shows a live countdown. Tap again to end early.

◉

+1 seed bonus per task

Every task completed during Focus earns 3 seeds instead of 2 — a flat +1 bonus. This stacks with the time-window bonus.

◉

One task visible

During Focus, only your first pending task is shown. This removes visual noise and forces sequential attention — the same principle that makes Pomodoro effective.

◉

Task input is locked

You cannot add new tasks while Focus is active. This prevents the common habit of adding tasks instead of completing them. New tasks can wait until the session ends.

◉

Auto-dismiss

When the timer ends, Focus deactivates automatically. A brief flash message confirms the session is complete. Normal mode resumes.

05 — Resilience Tokens

Earned, never bought

Tokens are not a premium currency. They cannot be purchased. They are awarded only as genuine milestones — recognition that you have done something consistently, not just something once.

What tokens do

Restore a broken streak

If your streak breaks because you missed a day, BloomDay offers you a recovery option if you have at least 1 token. The streak is restored, but the missed day's seeds are not. The cost is genuine.

Revive a critical plant

A plant that has reached Critical mood (4+ days unwatered) can be revived using 1 token without consuming a water drop. This is an emergency measure, not a shortcut.

06 — Weather

Real conditions, real effects

BloomDay reads your local weather once per day and applies it silently to your garden. Weather is not decorative — it has mechanical consequences.

Rain or Storm

Free watering for outdoor plants

Garden and allotment plants are automatically marked as watered. You do not spend water drops and no action is needed. A flash banner confirms it: Rain · Plants watered.

Hot days (>28°C)

Faster wilting for outdoor plants

On very hot days, garden and allotment plants lose mood faster if not watered. A subtle sky-tint overlay appears on your garden scene to signal the condition.

☁

The weather strip appears in the header bar next to the season indicator — a single line of text like ☁ Overcast · 14° London. It is fetched once per calendar day and cached. No constant location access is required.

07 — Garden

Arrange, tend, share

The Garden tab is a living scene of your plants and décor. You can switch between three environments — Houseplants, Garden, and Allotment — each with its own species and theme.

1

Switch environments

Use the three tabs at the top of the Garden screen to move between Houseplants (indoor living room), Garden (outdoor with house backdrop), and Allotment (vegetables and edibles). Each has its own plant roster and décor set.

2

Choose a theme

Each environment has multiple visual themes — Cosy, Greenhouse, Nordic for houseplants; English, Cottage, Walled for garden; and so on. Scroll the theme bar to switch. Some themes require a premium plan.

3

Arrange your space

Tap ARRANGE to enter edit mode. In this mode, plants and décor become draggable. Long-press to select an item. When selected, a REMOVE / CANCEL bar appears at the bottom — use it to delete plants or décor from your scene. Tap DONE to save.

4

Add décor

In ARRANGE mode, tap DECOR to reveal the décor shop. Items cost seeds and are placed immediately. Each environment has its own set: books and candles for indoors, benches and birdbaths for the garden, beehives and baskets for the allotment.

5

Share your garden

Tap SHARE GARDEN below the scene to capture a screenshot and open the share sheet. The image includes your plant names, streak, and the BloomDay watermark.

08 — Weekly Report

Seven days, clearly seen

The Weekly tab shows a summary of the past seven days: tasks completed, streak, watering consistency, daily challenge wins, category breakdown, and a bar chart of daily activity.

Mood headline

The report opens with a single mood line based on your completion rate — from "Outstanding week." to "Rest is growth too." It changes each week based on your actual rate.

The 2×2 stats grid

Four equal-sized cells: tasks done, day streak, days watered, and challenges won. Each has a coloured left accent matching its category. No orphaned pills, no wrapping asymmetry.

Daily bars

A compact bar chart shows each day's total vs. completed. A watering dot appears beneath days you watered at least one plant. Useful for spotting which days tend to go unfinished.

Category breakdown

Your top three task categories for the week are shown with colour-coded bars. Use this to notice imbalance — a week that is all Work and no Self, for instance.

09 — Tips & Good Habits

Getting the most from your garden

A few habits that make the system work for you rather than against you.

Water every day

Growth comes only from watering. Open the app each day, water your active plant, and let the streak build. A plant you forget to water for 4 days enters Critical — one token can revive it, but consistent care is always better.

Use Focus Mode for deep work

Every task completed during a Focus session earns +1 bonus seed. Use it for your hardest tasks — the locked input and single-task view removes the temptation to keep adding instead of doing.

Save for Rare plants

Don't spend seeds the moment you earn them. Forest-tier plants (200–280 seeds) take several weeks of consistent effort — that distance is intentional. Arriving there should feel genuinely earned.

Guard your streak

A streak only requires one completed task per day. On slow days, complete your smallest task just to keep the streak alive. The bonus seeds from a 14-day streak add up significantly over a month.

Daily Challenge completes itself

The Daily Challenge is checked automatically in the background. You do not need to tap anything. Complete your tasks and water your plants normally — the challenge reward appears as a flash message when you hit the target.

The app is designed around one principle: every system should be a consequence of real work, not a shortcut around it. The economy is tight by design. Rare plants are rare for a reason. Tokens cannot be bought. A full-bloom moment means you actually grew something.
